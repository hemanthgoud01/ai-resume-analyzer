Tailor any resume to a real job in minutes.

Drop in a resume, compare it against a target role or job description, and get a fast breakdown of ATS score, missing keywords, grammar issues, and high-impact improvements. This prototype runs fully in the browser and is ready to share.

What it helps with

Resume targeting, keyword alignment, quick polish, and a cleaner story for recruiters.

Keyword gaps Highlights missing role terms with practical suggestions.
Resume polish Catches readability, grammar, and formatting issues early.
